Chivda

Chivda is a delicious evening snack. Flattened rice flakes, tempered with aromatic curry leaves, groundnuts and dry fruits of your choice with light spices makes it a healthier choice.

Ingredients

  1. Thin Poha flakes: 2 cups
  2. Cumin seeds: 1/2 tablespoon
  3. Mustard seeds: 1/2 tablespoon
  4. Curry leaves (Chopped): 1/4 bunch
  5. Groundnuts: a handful
  6. Dry fruits: optional
  7. Red chili powder: to taste
  8. Turmeric
  9. Salt: to taste
  10. Oil: 2 Tablespoons

Procedure

  1. Lightly roast poha flakes in a pan.
  2. Separately shallow fry groundnuts and curry leaves. Leave them over poha after removing from flame.
  3. Sprinkle salt, chili powder, turmeric over the poha.
  4. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil and add Mustard seeds. Once the Mustard seeds splutter, add cumin seeds too.
  5. Spread the tempering over poha evenly and mix everything well.
  6. If using a pan for preparation, mix everything with a spatula.
  7. If using a container, you may shake well the contents with lid on to ensure nice mixture.
  8. Chivda is ready to serve!
