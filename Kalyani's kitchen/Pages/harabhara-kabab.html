Harabhara Kabab

Ingredients

  1. Boiled potatoes: 2
  2. Green peas: ½ cup
  3. Chopped Spinach/ Palak: ½ cup
  4. Chopped Coriander leaves: 2 Tablespoons
  5. Garam masala: ¼ tablespoon
  6. Fennel seeds powder: ¼ tablespoon
  7. Red chili powder: to taste
  8. Salt: to taste

Procedure

  1. Mix all the ingredients together to form a uniform batter.
  2. Make fine balls of kabab batter and arrange in the pan.
  3. Cook with lid covered from both sides.
  4. Hara Bhara kabab is ready to serve with Tomato ketchup.
